📌 About SPENDORA

SPENDORA Advanced is a full-stack personal finance management system built as a Final Year BSc Computer Science project. It helps users take complete control of their finances by tracking daily expenses, setting category-wise budgets, managing savings goals, and receiving AI-powered financial insights — all in one secure, responsive web application.
