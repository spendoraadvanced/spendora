📚 How It Works

Step 1: Set Your PIN

Create a 4-digit security PIN to protect your savings. This ensures only you can access your funds.

Step 2: Make Deposits

Add money to your savings bank anytime. You can deposit manually or set up automatic savings from expenses.

Step 3: Wait 3 Months

Your savings are locked for 3 months. This helps build discipline and prevents impulsive withdrawals.

Step 4: Unlock & Celebrate

After 3 months, your vault unlocks! You'll see your total savings in the dashboard.
